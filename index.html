Our Vision

Digital Media & Design is dedicated to establishing, advancing and promoting programs of trans-disciplinary education and research in digital media and design.Our commitment to experiential learning in the class room prepares our students to respond to real-world challenges that will distinguish them as thinkers and practitioners in the digital space. Students working in our state-of-the-art facilities work with industry standard hardware and software under the guidance of faculty who are all experienced professionals and practitioners. Each area of focus develops linkages to external industries who bring forward creative, intellectual, technological and/or production challenges that are addressed in class as part of our outcome driven commitment to education.

Interdisciplinary by Design

The Digital Media & Design Department has been built from the ground up to incorporate the expertise from multiple disciplines such as:

  • Business & entrepreneurship
  • Creative: digital art, digital music, temporal storytelling, and digital artifacts
  • Digital Humanities & Digital Social Sciences
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, Math
